I am currently a researcher in Zhejiang Airport Innovation Research Institute, Zhejiang Province Airport Group since 2023.07. I received my Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Zhejiang University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Guodao Sun (孙国道教授) and Prof. Ronghua Liang (梁荣华教授) at ZJUTVIS Group. I received my B.Sc degree in Automation from Zhejiang University of Technology. My main research interests include Dynamic Graph/Network Visualization, Traffic Data Visualization, and Sequential Data Visualization.
